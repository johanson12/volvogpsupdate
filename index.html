Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I update my Volvo GPS maps?

We recommend updating your Volvo GPS maps at least once per year. Map data changes frequently with new roads, businesses, and points of interest being added regularly. An annual Volvo GPS map update ensures you have the most current navigation information.

How long does a Volvo GPS map update take?

The time required for a Volvo GPS map update varies depending on your vehicle's system and the update method. USB updates typically take 30-60 minutes, while over-the-air updates may be faster. Your vehicle can typically be driven during the update process.

Will a map update improve my navigation system's performance?

Yes, a Volvo GPS map update can improve performance by providing more efficient routing algorithms, faster search capabilities, and better integration with your vehicle's other systems. However, it won't fix hardware issues with your navigation unit.
